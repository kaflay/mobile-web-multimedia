7 Days Mustang

Mustang District is one of the seventy-seven districts of Nepal. It covers an area of 3,573 km² and has a population of 13,452. The headquarters is Jomsom. The district is in Dhawalagiri Zone and part of Gandaki Pradesh in northern Nepal, straddles the Himalayas and extends northward onto the Tibetan Plateau

Outline

Day 01: Drive from Kathmandu to Beni./Jomsom

Day 02: Drive to Chhusang via Muktinath.

Day 03: Drive to Lo-manthang.

Day 04: Sightseeing in border, Gharphu cave and ancient monasteries.

Day 05: Drive from Lo-manthang to Jomsom/Marpha via Dumba lake.

Day 06: Drive to Pokhara.

Day 07: Drive to Kathmandu.
